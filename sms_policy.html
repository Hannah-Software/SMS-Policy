SMS Alerts Program

Paul Davis Restoration operates an internal SMS alert system to notify employees about critical operational events. This program is not customer-facing — messages are sent only to company employees who have opted in.

What Messages Are Sent

  • Pipeline Failure Alerts — Notifications when data extraction or processing systems encounter errors requiring attention.
  • Health Check Warnings — Alerts when operational data pipelines are overdue, returning errors, or showing anomalies.
  • Fleet Alerts — Critical vehicle maintenance notifications including overdue oil changes, expired registrations, recalls, and toll citations.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on operational events. Typical volume is 1–5 messages per week. During system issues, temporary spikes may occur but will not exceed 10 messages per day.

Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan.

Example Messages

System Alert
[OpsKPI] 2 pipeline(s) FAILED: compliance, xactanalysis. Check logs. Reply HELP for info, STOP to opt out.
Fleet Alert
[OpsKPI] Fleet: 3 critical alert(s) — oil_change, registration. Check dashboard. Reply HELP for info, STOP to opt out.

How to Opt In

During employee onboarding, team members are informed about the operational SMS alert system. Employees provide their mobile phone number and give documented consent to receive operational alerts. Enrollment is voluntary.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message at any time to unsubscribe from all operational alerts. You will receive a single confirmation message. No further messages will be sent after opting out.

Reply HELP to any message for support information and contact details.

Support

For questions about the SMS alert program, contact ops@devkhtxpdr.com.
